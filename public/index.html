features

TEXTS

simple messaging

Message your friends and family easily. WhatsUp uses your phone or computers internet connection to send messages in realtime.

PHOTOS AND VIDEOS

share moments that matter

Send photos and videos on WhatsUp instantly.

GROUP CHAT

Groups to keep in touch

Keep in touch with the groups of people that matter the most, like your family or coworkers. With group chats, you can share messages, photos, and videos with all of your friends at once.

DOCUMENTS

Document sharing made easy

Send PDFs, documents, spreadsheets, slideshows and more, without the hassle of email or file sharing apps.

WHATSUP ON WEB AND DESKTOP

Keep the Conversation Going

With WhatsUp on the web and desktop, you can seamlessly sync all of your chats to your computer so that you can chat on whatever device is most convenient.
